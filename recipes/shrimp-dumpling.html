Shrimp Dumpling

Description

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Dumpling
  • Cantonese Vegetable
  • Soup's seasoning
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Spring onion

Steps

  1. Place the shrimp on the dumpling and cover it
  2. Boil the water using the medium heat, put the dumpling in the boiled
  3. Wait until the dumpling is floating, then wait for additional 2-3 min
  4. Pick it up and rest it
  5. Boil the water using the medium heat, then add the soup seasoning, salt and black pepper, then add the Cantonese vegetable
  6. Wait until the vegetable is cooked, then add the dumpling add turn off the heat
  7. Add the spring onion and the dish is ready
