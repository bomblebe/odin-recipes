Crispy Pork Belly Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • Salt
  • Vinegar
  • Vegetable oil
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil the pork belly for 10 min
  2. Add 1 tbsp. of salt and 3 tbsp. of vinegar
  3. Wait until the water is boiled and flip the belly
  4. Continue to boil for 10 min, Rinse the pork with water
  5. Pick the pork skin with fork, doing this will help the pork cooked faster
  6. Slice the pork skin
  7. Spread the salt onto the pork skin
  8. Rinse the pork skin with the vinegar for 5 min
  9. Air-fry the pork belly until the skin is golden-brown and crispy
  10. Deep-fry the pork belly
  11. The crispy pork belly is ready to be served
