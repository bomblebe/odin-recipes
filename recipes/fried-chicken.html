Fried Chicken

Description

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken wing
  • 1 tbsp of fish sauce
  • 1/2 tsp of sugar
  • 1/2 tsp of pepper

Steps

  1. Rinse the chicken wing with water
  2. Fork the chicken wing
  3. Add the fish sauce, sugar, pepper to the chicken wing
  4. Leave in the fridge for 30 min - 1 hour
  5. Heat the oil, fried the marinated chicken wing
  6. Wait until the chicken wing is golden brown, stop the frying and drain the excess oil in the paper
  7. The dish is ready !!!
